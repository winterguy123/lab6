What is the Poison Hemlock?

Where is the Hemlock?

Poison Hemlock can typically be found in sunny areas, fields, and ditches. Hemlock plants have red/purple spots and streaks, and often grow up to 10 feet tall with small white 5 petal flowers.

What does the Hemlock do?

Hemlock plants are very toxic. Even a small amount of hemlock being consumed can spell death to humans. If the plant is consumed, contact authorities quickly.
